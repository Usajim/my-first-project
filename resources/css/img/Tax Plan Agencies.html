Business Advisory Services

We provide Business Advisory Services to profit and non-profit making organizations.Following are some of Business Advisory Services we offer: -

  • Maintenance of books of accounts
  • Preparation of management and final financial statements
  • Preparation of cash flow forecasts
  • Preparation of payroll and filing of related statutory returns
  • Preparation and filing of VAT returns
  • Reconciliation of financial records
  • Preparation of budgets, budgetary controls, budget monitoring and variance analysis
  • Fixed Assets Register development and maintenance
  • Computerization of accounting systems
